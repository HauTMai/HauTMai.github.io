Background

Hau T. Mai graduated with a PhD in the Deep Learning Architecture Research Center (DLARC) in the Architectural Engineering at Sejong University (SJU) in 2023, under supervision of Prof. Jaehong Lee. He was one year of a postdoctoral fellowship here. His researches focus on the application of artificial intelligence for the analysis and optimization of structures. Especially, his favorite research direction is the physics-informed neural network (PINN) for solving mechanics problems without using any simulation numerical, such as finite element analysis, finite different analysis, and so on. Now, he is a lecturer at the Faculty of Mechanical Technology , Industrial University of Ho Chi Minh City , VietNam.
